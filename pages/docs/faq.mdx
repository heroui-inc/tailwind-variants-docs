<h2>How do I enable autocompletion in my IDE?</h2>
<p>To enable autocompletion inside <code>tv</code> in your favourite IDE, use the same method as for <code>cva</code> which is explained <a href="https://github.com/joe-bell/cva/blob/f45fdafc2031e82e10ef4aba3bd8e64351bc7b2e/docs/pages/docs/installation.mdx#intellisense" target="_blank" className="underline">here</a>. This means adding <code>classRegex</code> to your TailwindCSS settings. In WebStorm, you can find this under the Tailwind CSS page in Settings. In Visual Studio Code, add it to your to your <a href="https://code.visualstudio.com/docs/getstarted/settings" target="_blank" className="underline"><code>settings.json</code></a>.</p>
